# RoolsVideoCUT

RoolsVideoCUT is a self-hosted desktop tool that automatically generates short-form highlight clips from a creator's own gameplay recordings and uploads them to the creator's own social media accounts (e.g. YouTube, TikTok) via the respective official APIs.

This page hosts the legal documents required by the platforms RoolsVideoCUT integrates with.
