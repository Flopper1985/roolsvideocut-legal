## 1. Overview

RoolsVideoCUT ("the Software") is a self-hosted desktop application that processes a user's own video recordings on the user's own hardware and, at the user's request, uploads the resulting clips to social-media platforms (e.g. TikTok, YouTube) through those platforms' official APIs.

The Software is operated locally by the user. There is no hosted service, no shared backend, and no third-party user base. Each installation is operated by a single individual on their own computer.
